# Aave

## Definition

**Aave** refers to the pattern of decentralized lending protocols that enable users to lend and borrow cryptocurrencies without intermediaries, providing liquidity and earning capabilities, often through blockchain technology, tokenization, and decentralized governance systems.

## Core Concepts

- **Aave**: Decentralized lending protocol
- **Lending**: Lending cryptocurrencies
- **Borrowing**: Borrowing cryptocurrencies
- **Liquidity**: Providing liquidity
- **Decentralized**: Not controlled by central authority
