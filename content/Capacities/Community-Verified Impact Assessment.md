# Community-Verified Impact Assessment

## Definition

**Community-Verified Impact Assessment** is the capacity of blockchain systems to enable communities to collectively measure, verify, and assess the social and environmental impact of projects, initiatives, and interventions. This allows for decentralized impact measurement that respects local knowledge, values, and context while providing transparent and auditable impact data.

## Core Concepts

- **Community Control**: Local communities control impact assessment processes
- **Local Knowledge**: Leveraging local expertise and understanding
- **Transparent Measurement**: Open and auditable impact measurement
- **Contextual Assessment**: Impact assessment that respects local context
- **Participatory Methods**: Community participation in impact assessment
