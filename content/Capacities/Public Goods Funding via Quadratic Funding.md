# Public Goods Funding via Quadratic Funding

## Definition

**Public Goods Funding via Quadratic Funding** is the capacity of blockchain systems to enable more efficient and democratic funding of public goods through quadratic funding mechanisms, where the impact of funding is maximized by considering both the number of contributors and the amount contributed.

## Core Concepts

- **Quadratic Funding**: Funding mechanism that considers both number of contributors and amount contributed
- **Public Goods**: Goods that benefit everyone and cannot be excluded from
- **Democratic Funding**: Funding decisions made by the community
- **Impact Maximization**: Maximizing the impact of funding
- **Community Participation**: Community participation in funding decisions
