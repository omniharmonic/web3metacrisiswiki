# Data Sovereignty

## Definition

**Data Sovereignty** refers to the pattern of individuals and communities having control over their own data, including the right to access, use, and share their data, often through blockchain technology, tokenization, and decentralized governance systems.

## Core Concepts

- **Data Sovereignty**: Control over data
