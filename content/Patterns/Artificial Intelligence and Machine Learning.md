# Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

## Definition

**Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning** refers to the development and deployment of computer systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human intelligence, including learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making, often with significant implications for privacy, autonomy, and social control.

## Core Concepts

- **Machine Learning**: Algorithms that learn from data to make predictions or decisions
- **Artificial Intelligence**: Computer systems that simulate human intelligence
- **Data Processing**: Large-scale processing of personal and behavioral data
- **Automated Decision Making**: AI-driven automated decisions affecting individuals
- **Privacy Violation**: Massive invasion of personal privacy through data collection
- **Autonomy Erosion**: Undermining human autonomy and decision-making

## Technical Mechanisms

### AI and ML Technologies
- **Neural Networks**: Deep learning and artificial neural networks
- **Natural Language Processing**: AI-powered text and language analysis
- **Computer Vision**: AI-powered image and video analysis
- **Predictive Analytics**: Using data to predict future behavior and events
- **Recommendation Systems**: AI-powered content and product recommendations
- **Automated Decision Making**: AI-driven automated decisions

### Data Collection and Processing

- **Cross-Platform Integration**: Integration across multiple systems
