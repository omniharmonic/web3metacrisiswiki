# Availability Heuristic

## Definition

**Availability Heuristic** refers to the cognitive bias where people judge the probability or frequency of events based on how easily examples come to mind, often leading to distorted perceptions of reality and decision-making errors.

## Core Concepts

- **Cognitive Bias**: Systematic errors in thinking
- **Availability**: Ease of recalling examples
- **Heuristics**: Mental shortcuts for decision-making
- **Perception**: How we perceive reality
- **Decision-Making**: How we make decisions
