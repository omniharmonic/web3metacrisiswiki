# Algorithmic Amplification

## Definition

**Algorithmic Amplification** refers to the pattern of using algorithmic systems to amplify certain content, messages, or behaviors beyond their natural reach, often leading to disproportionate influence and unintended consequences in social and political systems.

## Core Concepts

- **Amplification**: Increasing the reach and impact of content
- **Algorithmic Systems**: Automated systems for content distribution
- **Viral Content**: Content that spreads rapidly
- **Influence**: Disproportionate influence on public discourse
- **Unintended Consequences**: Unexpected outcomes from amplification
