# Chilling Effects

## Definition

**Chilling Effects** refer to the phenomenon where individuals and groups self-censor their speech, behavior, and activities due to fear of surveillance, monitoring, or negative consequences, leading to a reduction in free expression, creativity, and democratic participation.

## Core Concepts

- **Self-Censorship**: Individuals voluntarily restricting their own speech and behavior
- **Fear of Surveillance**: Fear of being monitored or watched
- **Fear of Consequences**: Fear of negative consequences for speech or behavior
- **Reduced Expression**: Reduction in free expression and creativity
- **Democratic Erosion**: Undermining democratic participation and discourse
- **Social Control**: Enabling social control through fear and intimidation
