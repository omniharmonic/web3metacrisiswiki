
## Definition

**Regenerative Economics** refers to the pattern of economic systems that prioritize the regeneration of natural and social capital over extraction and depletion, often through blockchain technology, tokenization, and decentralized governance systems.

## Core Concepts

- **Regenerative Economics**: Economic systems that regenerate capital
- **Natural Capital**: Natural resources and ecosystems
- **Social Capital**: Social relationships and institutions
- **Regeneration**: Restoring and improving capital
- **Extraction**: Taking resources without replacement
