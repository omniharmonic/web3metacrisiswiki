# Authoritarian Technology

## Definition

**Authoritarian Technology** refers to technological systems and tools designed to enable, facilitate, or enhance authoritarian control, surveillance, censorship, and social manipulation, often with significant implications for democracy, human rights, and individual freedom.

## Core Concepts

- **Authoritarian Control**: Technology designed to enable authoritarian control
- **Surveillance**: Comprehensive surveillance and monitoring capabilities
- **Censorship**: Technology designed to enable censorship and information control
- **Social Manipulation**: Technology designed to manipulate social behavior
- **Democracy Erosion**: Undermining democratic processes and institutions
- **Human Rights Violations**: Violating fundamental human rights

- **Location Tracking**: GPS and location-based monitoring
