# Commons Governance

## Definition

**Commons Governance** refers to the pattern of governing shared resources and common goods through decentralized, community-based decision-making processes, often implemented through blockchain technology and token-based systems.

## Core Concepts

- **Commons Governance**: Governance of shared resources
- **Community Decision-Making**: Community-based decision-making
- **Decentralized Governance**: Decentralized governance systems
- **Blockchain**: Blockchain-based commons governance
- **Tokenization**: Tokenizing commons governance
