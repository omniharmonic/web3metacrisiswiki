# Network Nations

## Definition

**Network Nations** refer to the emergence of decentralized, borderless digital communities and governance structures that transcend traditional nation-state boundaries, enabled by blockchain technology, decentralized governance mechanisms, and digital identity systems.

## Core Concepts

- **Digital Sovereignty**: Self-governing digital communities
- **Borderless Governance**: Governance without geographical boundaries
- **Digital Citizenship**: Citizenship in digital communities
- **Network Governance**: Governance through network effects
- **Decentralized Autonomy**: Autonomous decentralized organizations
- **Digital Rights**: Rights in digital communities
