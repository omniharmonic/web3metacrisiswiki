# Ecosystem Services

## Definition

**Ecosystem Services** refers to the pattern of recognizing, valuing, and monetizing the benefits that ecosystems provide to humans, often through blockchain technology, tokenization, and decentralized governance systems.

## Core Concepts

- **Ecosystem Services**: Benefits provided by ecosystems
- **Valuation**: Assigning value to ecosystem services
- **Monetization**: Converting ecosystem services into monetary value
- **Blockchain**: Blockchain-based ecosystem service systems
- **Tokenization**: Tokenizing ecosystem services
