# Confirmation Bias

## Definition

**Confirmation Bias** refers to the cognitive bias where people seek, interpret, and remember information in a way that confirms their existing beliefs, often leading to distorted perceptions of reality and resistance to new information.

## Core Concepts

- **Cognitive Bias**: Systematic errors in thinking
- **Confirmation**: Seeking confirming information
- **Bias**: Systematic bias in thinking
- **Perception**: How we perceive reality
- **Decision-Making**: How we make decisions
