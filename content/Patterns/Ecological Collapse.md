# Ecological Collapse

## Definition

**Ecological Collapse** refers to the pattern of environmental degradation and ecosystem breakdown that occurs when natural systems are pushed beyond their capacity to recover, often exacerbated by economic and social systems that fail to account for environmental externalities.

## Core Concepts

- **Ecological Collapse**: Breakdown of natural systems
- **Environmental Degradation**: Degradation of the environment
- **Ecosystem Breakdown**: Breakdown of ecosystems
- **Externalities**: Environmental externalities
- **Recovery**: System recovery capacity
